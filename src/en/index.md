## AAA Concept

MeetEasy is a pioneering **2B2C** smart conference SaaS platform built around the **AAA Intelligent Conference Concept**:

* **🤖 A - AI Programming & Development**
  The entire MeetEasy system is **fully developed and maintained by AI**, ensuring rapid evolution and responsiveness. It natively supports **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** for automated conference information management and intelligent Q&A services, and is fully integrated with the **MCP (Model Context Protocol)** tool ecosystem. Through standardized tool interfaces, AI Agents can directly and securely interact with conference agendas, registrations, and other system resources, unlocking the full potential of AI-driven automation.

* **💼 A - AI Management & Console**
  For **B-side conference organizers**, the Console leverages AI to simplify operations. It supports **AI OCR recognition of conference handbooks** (PDFs/images) to **automatically import and structure conference information** (agendas, speaker lists, hotel allocations), eliminating manual data entry. Organizers can also describe their requirements in natural language to automatically generate conference microsites in VisuSpace with corresponding registration forms and schedules, backed by real-time analytics.

* **📱 A - AI Service & MeetApp**
  For **C-side conference attendees**, MeetApp and WeChat mini programs provide an embeddable AI Conference Chatbox. Leveraging speech-to-text (ASR) and a tailored LLM, the Chatbox answers attendee questions in real time, provides automatic agenda reminders, seat lookups, and personalized recommendations, acting as a personal concierge.
