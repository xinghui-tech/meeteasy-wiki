# MeetApp — Getting Started

**MeetApp** is the attendee-facing mobile web application. Use it to browse conference microsites, register, interact with the AI assistant, check in, and find your seat.

## How to Access MeetApp

| Method | Steps |
|--------|-------|
| **Direct link** | Open the conference URL shared by the organizer (email, SMS, social media) |
| **QR code** | Scan the conference QR code at the venue or on promotional materials |
| **WeChat** | Open the **WeApp** mini program and select the conference |

MeetApp works best on mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Android Chrome) and inside WeChat WebView.

## First Visit

1. Open the conference link.
2. The VisuSpace microsite homepage loads with conference info, countdown, and navigation.
3. Browse pages: agenda, speakers, venue, registration.

## Sign In (If Required)

Some conferences require login before registration or AI chat:

1. Tap **Sign In** or attempt an action that requires authentication.
2. Log in with phone, email, or WeChat (in WeApp).
3. Your session persists for the duration of the conference.

## Navigation

The microsite navigation is configured by the organizer in VisuSpace:

- **Home** — Conference overview and countdown
- **Agenda** — Session schedule and details
- **Speakers** — Speaker profiles and bios
- **Register** — Registration form
- **Check-in** — On-site check-in (available during the event)
- **AI Assistant** — Chat with the conference AI helper

## Language & Appearance

- MeetApp supports **English** and **Chinese** — switch via the language selector if available.
- Light and dark themes follow your device settings or can be toggled manually.

## Tips

- Add the conference page to your phone's home screen for quick access
- Enable notifications if prompted — you'll receive session reminders and updates
- Save your ticket code after registration for fast check-in
- Use the AI assistant to find sessions, speakers, and venue information
