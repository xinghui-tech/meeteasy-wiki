# Check-in & Seat Lookup

Check in to your conference on event day and find your assigned seat in MeetApp.

## Before Event Day

Make sure you have:

- **Approved registration** — Check your status on the conference page
- **Ticket code** — Save the code from your registration confirmation
- **MeetApp link** — Bookmark or add to home screen for quick access

## Self Check-in

1. Open the conference in MeetApp on event day.
2. Navigate to **Check-in** from the menu or homepage button.
3. Enter your **ticket code** or tap **Show QR Code**.
4. Submit — you'll see a confirmation message.

## QR Code Check-in

For faster check-in at staffed stations:

1. Open **Check-in** in MeetApp.
2. Tap **Show QR Code** to display your personal QR code.
3. Present the QR code to event staff who scan it with Console.

## Staff-Assisted Check-in

If self check-in is not enabled or you need help:

1. Provide your name, email, or ticket code to event staff.
2. Staff checks you in using Console.
3. Your MeetApp status updates to "Checked in" automatically.

## Seat Lookup

If the organizer has configured seating:

1. After check-in, your **seat number** appears on the confirmation screen.
2. Tap **View Seat Map** to see your seat highlighted on the venue layout.
3. You can also find seat info in your registration details before check-in (if the organizer enabled pre-assignment display).

## Check-in Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Not checked in | Awaiting check-in on event day |
| Checked in | Successfully checked in; timestamp recorded |
| Check-in closed | Check-in window has ended |

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Ticket code not recognized | Verify code from registration confirmation; contact event staff |
| Check-in button not visible | Check-in may not be open yet — wait until the check-in window starts |
| Seat not shown | Seating may not be configured, or assignment happens at check-in — ask event staff |
| QR code won't display | Ensure registration is approved; refresh the page |

## Tips

- Screenshot your ticket code before arriving at the venue
- Arrive early during peak check-in times to avoid queues
- If using QR check-in, increase screen brightness for reliable scanning
- Save the seat map screenshot if venue Wi-Fi is unreliable
