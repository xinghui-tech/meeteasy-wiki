## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: AAA concept on homepage
The homepage SHALL include a clear section explaining the AAA concept tailored for the 2B2C product "星汇小蜜书": AI Programming (Ai开发), AI Management (Ai管理) for B-side customers, and AI Service (Ai服务) for C-side users.

#### Scenario: Visitor views the homepage
- **WHEN** a visitor loads the wiki homepage (`src/index.md`)
- **THEN** they SHALL see a prominent section detailing the AAA concept and how it shapes the product's architecture and value proposition

### Requirement: AI Programming detailed definition
The explanation of "Ai编程（开发）" in the AAA section SHALL explicitly state that the entire system is developed and maintained by AI, natively supports RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) for conference information management and intelligent services, and integrates MCP (Model Context Protocol) tool ecosystems.

#### Scenario: Developer reviews AI Programming details
- **WHEN** a developer or prospective customer views the AAA section on the homepage
- **THEN** they SHALL see clear details specifying how AI maintains the codebase, how RAG provides smart conference Q&A, and how MCP extends the capability of AI agents
